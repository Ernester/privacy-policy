Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2024

Turbo Cleaner AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Our Core Privacy Promise: Turbo Cleaner AI processes all files and data locally on your device. We do NOT upload your personal files, photos, or documents to any cloud servers.

1. Information We Collect

To provide device cleaning and optimization services, we access the following information:

  • Media and Files: We scan your device's storage to identify duplicate photos, large videos, and temporary files. This is done locally to provide cleaning recommendations.
  • Installed Application Information: We collect a list of installed apps and their storage footprint to help you identify which apps are consuming the most space.
  • Device Information: Basic info such as device model, OS version, and system language.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party SDKs that may collect information used to identify you or track app performance:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your advertising ID and usage data to show relevant ads. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): Used to monitor app stability and user engagement. It helps us fix bugs and improve the user experience. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process premium subscriptions. We do not store your credit card or financial information; all transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

3. Permissions Usage

To function correctly, the app requires the following sensitive permissions:

  • Storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA): Required to scan for junk files and duplicates.
  • Usage Access: Required for the "App Analyzer" feature to calculate the storage size of each installed application.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Since your personal files are never transmitted to our servers, the risk of data breach is significantly minimized. For technical data collected by Firebase/AdMob, we use industry-standard encryption.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
